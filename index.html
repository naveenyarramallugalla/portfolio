The platform provides real-time tracking of stock levels, ensuring that inventory is properly managed and recorded. Calmscient

CalmScient is a mobile and admin application designed to support individuals dealing with anxiety and depression. The mobile app provides resources for mental wellness, meditation, mood tracking, and access to professional help. The admin panel offers management capabilities for healthcare providers to monitor patient progress, schedule sessions, and provide personalized care. I contributed by designing the user interfaces, ensuring the application was simple, calming, and accessible to all users.

Teachopia

Teachopia is a free experiential learning app developed by Caring with Colour for teachers in Indian public schools. It offers NCERT-aligned lesson plans, videos, worksheets, and project-based modules for Grades 4–7 in Math, Science, and English. The app supports offline access, making it ideal for schools with limited connectivity. It also includes in-app teacher training aligned with NEP 2020 to boost teaching quality.
